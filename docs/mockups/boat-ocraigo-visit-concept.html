Getting here

At the gateway to the Black Spur.

Arriving by car

Boat O'Craigo is approximately a three-minute drive from central Healesville at 458 Maroondah Highway. On-site parking is available.

Arriving by bus

Public bus services operate from Lilydale Station toward Healesville. Confirm the current route and timetable before travelling.

Book a tour operator

Prefer to leave the driving to someone else? A range of local operators visit wineries throughout the Yarra Valley.
