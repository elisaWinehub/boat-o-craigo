Frequently asked questions

Answers before you need to ask.

Cellar Door & Restaurant

The Cellar Door is generally open Thursday to Monday from 10am to 5pm, with tastings available until 4pm. Restaurant service times vary between weekdays and weekends.
Walk-ins may be accepted when space allows, but bookings are recommended—especially on weekends and public holidays.
Most common dietary requirements can be accommodated with notice. Add them during booking or contact the team before your visit.

Wine Club & Our Wines

Members can update account information through the customer portal or contact the Wine Club team for assistance.
Some wines may be suitable for vegan customers. Final production copy should be confirmed against current winemaking and product information.
Store wine in a cool, dark place with a consistent temperature. Bottles sealed with cork should generally be stored on their side.

Orders & Delivery

Yes. Gift vouchers and gift products can be purchased through the online store, subject to the final Shopify catalogue setup.
Delivery timing depends on the destination and fulfilment schedule. Order confirmation and tracking information will be sent by email.
Photograph the damaged packaging and products, keep the items, and contact the team with your order number so they can assist.
